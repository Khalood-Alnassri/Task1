artificial intelligence?
- what does artificial intelligence mean?
Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a field of computer science that focuses on creating machines or software that can think, learn, and make decisions in ways that normally require human intelligence.

- Types of artificial intelligence and deferent between them?
Types of Artificial Intelligence:
1.Narrow AI:
-Designed to perform one specific task.
-Works within limited conditions.
-Cannot think or reason like a human.
Examples:
Chatbots, voice assistants, face recognition, recommendation systems.
2.General AI:
-Can understand, learn, and apply intelligence like a human.
-Can perform any intellectual task.
-Does not exist yet.
Example:
A machine that can learn any subject like a human.
3.Super AI:
-Intelligence that exceeds human intelligence.
-Can outperform humans in creativity and decision-making.
-Only theoretical / science fiction.
Example:
JARVIS (from Iron Man).
 
| Feature              | Narrow AI      | General AI  | Super AI           |
| -------------------- | -------------- | ----------- | ------------------ |
| Intelligence level   | Task-specific  | Human-level | Above human        |
| Learning ability     | Limited        | Broad       | Extremely advanced |
| Self-awareness       | No             | Possibly    | Yes                |
| Real-world existence | Yes            | No          | No                 |
| Examples             | Siri, chatbots | None        | Fictional          |
